Cedar Grove Community Park

A shared space for recreation, relaxation, and community.

About Our Park

The Cedar Grove Community Park is a shared neighborhood space maintained by residents and the HOA. It provides an area for families, children, and neighbors to enjoy the outdoors and spend time together.

We encourage everyone to help keep the park clean, safe, and enjoyable for future generations.

Park Rules

  • Park hours are dawn to dusk.
  • Children should be supervised by an adult.
  • Please clean up after yourself.
  • Pets must remain under control and waste must be removed.
  • No vandalism or damage to park property.
  • Respect your neighbors and keep noise at reasonable levels.
  • Follow all HOA community guidelines while using the park.

These rules can be updated as needed by the HOA Board.

Help Care For Our Park

The park belongs to all of us. Volunteers help with cleanup, maintenance, landscaping, playground improvements, and special projects throughout the year.

Ways You Can Help

  • Participate in community cleanup days.
  • Help with landscaping and beautification.
  • Report maintenance concerns.
  • Volunteer for park improvement projects.
  • Help organize family-friendly community events.

Questions or Suggestions?

If you have ideas for improving the park or would like to volunteer, please contact the HOA Board.
